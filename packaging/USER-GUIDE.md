# Welcome to Noteration 📘

Noteration turns your study PDFs into clean notes, quizzes, and flashcards, and
builds a revision schedule for you. Everything runs **on your own computer** — your
files never leave your machine.

This guide takes about 5 minutes. No technical knowledge needed.

---

## 1. Install it

### Windows

1. Double-click **`Noteration-Setup.exe`**.
2. You may see a blue **“Windows protected your PC”** box. This is normal for new
   apps. Click **More info**, then **Run anyway**.
3. Follow the installer (just keep clicking **Next**). It installs for you only and
   doesn’t need an administrator password.
4. When it finishes, open **Noteration** from your Desktop or the Start menu.

### Mac

1. Double-click **`Noteration-macOS.dmg`**.
2. Drag the **Noteration** icon onto the **Applications** folder shown in the window.
3. Open **Applications**, then **right-click** Noteration and choose **Open**
   (do this the first time only — a normal double-click may be blocked).
4. A box says it’s from an unidentified developer — click **Open**.

> The warnings appear only because the app isn’t registered with Microsoft/Apple
> yet. It’s safe — everything runs locally on your computer.

---

## 2. Add your free AI key (one time)

Noteration uses Google’s **free** Gemini AI to write your notes. You need a key —
it’s free and takes two minutes.

1. Go to **https://aistudio.google.com/apikey** and sign in with a Google account.
2. Click **Create API key**, then **copy** the key it gives you.
3. In Noteration, open **Settings** (top of the window) and paste the key into the
   **Gemini API key** box. Click **Save**.

That’s it — you’re ready. (The free tier is enough for regular studying. You never
have to enter a credit card.)

---

## 3. Use it

1. Click **Upload PDF** and pick a lecture, textbook chapter, or slide deck.
2. Noteration shows the chapters/topics it found — adjust if you like, then
   **Confirm**.
3. It starts generating in the background. Watch progress on the **Processing**
   page; topics turn **ready** as they finish (you can start studying the ready
   ones right away).
4. Open a topic to read the **Notes**, take the **Quiz**, and flip the
   **Flashcards**. Grade yourself on the cards and Noteration schedules your
   reviews on the **Calendar**.

---

## Good to know

- **Your data** (notes, database, uploaded files) is stored here, and is kept even
  if you uninstall:
  - Windows: `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Noteration`
  - Mac: `~/Library/Application Support/Noteration`
  To back up or move to a new computer, copy that folder.
- **Closing the window** fully closes the app.
- **Something went wrong?** There’s a file called **`noteration.log`** in the data
  folder above. Send it along if you need help — it explains what happened.

Happy studying! 🎓
